resist.guide

Intended as an open source of information for individuals coordinating mutual aid.

The Covid-19 outbreak presents an opportunity for us to help one another.

We agree that humans deserve basic rights,
and humanitarian aid is desparately needed by the people,
regardless of what big governments are squabbling about these days.

In solidarity with the rent, debt, and labour strikes,
we are calling for an organized general strike within your community.

Our organization effort resides in the Cascadia Bioregion.
Please ask your local labour union about participating safely.

Our team focus during this crisis is developing secure communications and collaboration software.

If you grok git version control, we could use your skills.

We need documentation, QA/testing/debugging, and software developers.
