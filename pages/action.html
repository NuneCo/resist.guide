Taking Action

Communication is a core need of organization.

Development happens on gitea,
discussion happens on discord,
and everything else is an email.

In an ideal world,
we'd reach through the isolation
to help one another out.

This printables directory may help with that.

In this world,
we have some partial solutions,
but none that quite work "well".

If you already have a secure workflow,
let us know,
and we'll adjust.

Accessible communication is a core goal.

Coordinated actions:
1) April First - General Strike. * one year long debt, labour, and travel moratorium. * dissolution of federal government, essential services defined and maintained by states. * regional logistics audit for healthcare, .

2) April Second - Rent Strike. * for those who have no option. * for the communities we live among. * for fairness and equality in housing.

3) Right Now - Mutual Aid * help your neighbor * use extra resources and ability to create community * commit to resisting the strain this crisis places on our families.
